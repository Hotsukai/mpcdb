Music Critique Database Readme

Naming Rule:
For Recording Data
PieceID-PlayerID.wav
e.g. perfomance of beethoven's piece by player 1
n01-bee-sym3-p01.wav

For Critique Data
PieceID-PlayerID-CriticID.wav
e.g. commentary by critique 1 on perfomance of beethoven's piece by player 1
n01-bee-sym3-p01-c03.txt
